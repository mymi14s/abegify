Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1st, 2026

Abegify values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Abegify platform.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Email address or phone number
  • User profile preferences
  • Incident reports, media uploads, and verification votes

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Location data (GPS-based, when enabled)
  • Device and app usage data
  • Log and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide location-based safety alerts
  • To display nearby incidents and trends
  • To enable community verification
  • To improve platform performance and safety

3. Location Data

Location data is essential to Abegify’s core functionality. You may limit or disable location access, but this may reduce functionality.

4. Media and Sensitive Content

Uploaded media may be automatically processed to blur faces or anonymise voices. You should avoid uploading content that exposes victims unnecessarily.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share anonymised, aggregated data with partners for research, safety analysis, or public interest purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for safety, analytics, and legal compliance. You may request deletion of your account and personal data.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption, access controls, and monitoring to protect your data.

8. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion
  • Control notification preferences

9. Children’s Privacy

Abegify is strictly intended for all ages. Our safety reporting services are not directed at, or designed for, children or minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a minor, we will take immediate steps to delete that information.

10. International Use

Abegify operates globally. Data may be processed in jurisdictions different from your location, in compliance with applicable laws.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of Abegify indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
